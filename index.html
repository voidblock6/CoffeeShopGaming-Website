Welcome to the CoffeeShopGaming website

CoffeeShopGaming was originally founded as a Minecraft server for people from an indie-game Discord server. We eventually grew and rebranded into the CoffeeShopGaming you know today, a tiny but vibrant community full of amazing people.

The Server

Season 2 is currently running! It's a Forge 1.20.1 modpack built around Create mod and Origins(++). We have a minigame area featuring spleef, TNT run, and more on the way. We also host bi-weekly events. Our most recent ones were the End Fight and a custom parkour with a prize. Check out the picture library to see some screenshots from the server.
